The cleanest stat on my resume is also the one I'm most careful about.
Somewhere in my FPT years we reduced production issues by roughly 30%. Here
is what that number actually meant, and what I've been using it to argue
ever since.

## It wasn't a testing win

The temptation is to credit the test suite. That's not what happened. The
30% came from moving the conversation earlier — getting test cases written
during spec review instead of after development.
