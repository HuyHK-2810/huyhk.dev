---

The first time I shipped OAuth/SSO for an enterprise tenant I assumed it would
be a long afternoon. It was three weeks. The protocol is the easy part. The
hard part is everything around it — clock skew, certificate rotation, attribute
mapping disagreements between IdPs, and customers who configured their own IdP
years ago and no longer remember how.

## What I'd tell past me

Start with a single tenant in staging. Build the unhappy-path UI first. And
treat metadata expiry as a Sev-3 you forgot to schedule.
